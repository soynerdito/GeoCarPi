GeoCar Pi

The GeoCarPi is nothing more than a Raspberry Pi installed in a car with a GPS, WIFI and expandable Intenet of Things (IoT) plugins for expandable support.

Still project is not running, not even in beta. Adding little by little and hope to have it soon running in my car.
